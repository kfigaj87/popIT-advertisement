What is Pop It?

"Pop It" is a popular fidget toy consisting of a grid of small raised bubbles that can be pressed and popped. It provides auditory and sensory stimulation, helping to reduce stress and tension. It is available in various shapes, colors, and sizes, and is enjoyed by both children and adults.

Rules of the game

In the game "Pop It," your goal is to "pop" all the bubbles on the board. Each popper has small raised bubbles that you can press. When you press a bubble, it makes a sound and the bubble pops up. You can press the bubbles individually or in groups. The game ends when all the bubbles are popped. You can try to beat your own record time or invite others to compete. "Pop It" is a sensory toy that provides entertainment and sensory stimulation. The rules of the game can be customized based on players' preferences and creativity.
